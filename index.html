About

In times of hardship, uncertainty and fear, community is more important than ever. We rely on those around us for solidarity, support, guidance, information and empathy. One of the greatest challenges of the COVID-19 epidemic is that to slow the spread of the virus we must practice social distancing and forgo in-person community. But there is a reason for optimism: community and social distancing are not incompatible. Thanks to the internet, we can maintain and grow the bonds of community among us even while physically quarantined and isolated. At Smiling Minds, online community is central to our mission and vision.

Join us in making learning more fun! We connect enthusiastic language learners like you to native speakers to bring about a feeling of togetherness and belonging; promoting diversity and inclusivity along the way!
